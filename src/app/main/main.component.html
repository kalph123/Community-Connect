About Community Connect

Community Connect is a complete digital transformation for organizations, associations and non-profits to experience fully engaged community through cloud based software platform. The platform comes with Android and iOS mobile apps fully customizable to meet the need of each organization. Community Connect can seamlessly take an organization to digital transformation using it proven event management portal that only allows to host the events but also allows to steer the event on the day of the event.
